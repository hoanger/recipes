Ingredients:
 - 1 packet of instant nooles
 	- 1 piece of dry noodle
 	- 1 seasoning pack
 	- 1 freeze dried "vegetables"

Directions:
 1. Bring water to boil.
 2. Add noodles and bil for 3-5 minutes.
 3. Strain nooles. (This will keep your noodles tasting better than just adding to the boiling water)
 4. Add seasoning pack and "vegetables" to a bowl with 250mL of hot water. Let sit for 3 minutes.
 5. Add strained noodles and fill with hot water to desired level/taste. Mix and Enjoy!
